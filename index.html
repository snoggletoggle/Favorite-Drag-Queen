Help

General

This presents you with 2 queens to choose from. Click (or hit the corresponding arrow key) to select the Queen you prefer. You can choose any queen for any reason, your preferences are your own.

Choosing Seasons

You can choose any combination of seasons to compare queens from. Once you start the game, you can no longer pick different seasons. Refreshing the page will allow you to pick again.
